Product & Technology Innovation

Module Assessment & Prioritization Process

How new module ideas move from concept to roadmap — the structured pathway for engaging our product and technology innovation team.

1
Submission
Product Intake Form

Any team across the organization can submit a new module idea via the SharePoint intake form. The form captures the concept, the business problem it addresses, and the submitter's initial perspective on market opportunity. This ensures every idea enters the pipeline through a single, consistent channel.

2
Evaluation
Weekly Innovation Review

Each week, the Product & Technology Innovation team reviews all new submissions. We meet directly with every submitter to scope out the idea's feasibility and business value, ensuring we fully understand the problem space before making any assessment.

3
Decision Gate
Market Value Assessment

The team evaluates whether the idea presents a strong market value proposition. This is the first critical gate — it determines whether the idea advances to a full business case or is redirected.

Strong market value proposition?
✓ Yes — Advance
Proceed to Module Business Case development with the innovation team.
✗ Not Yet
Feedback provided to submitter. Idea logged for future re-evaluation as market conditions evolve.
4
Business Case Development
Module Business Case Template

For ideas that clear the market value gate, the team completes a structured business case built around three pillars that together provide the full picture of viability, return potential, and commercial readiness. Access the template to get started.

1
Module Overview & Objective
Clear articulation of what the module does, the problem it solves, and the strategic objective it serves within the broader platform.
Value Proposition Company Impact Workflows Enabled
2
Anticipated ROI & Weighted Scoring
Quantified return-on-investment analysis using weighted scoring criteria across four dimensions.
Financial Model Feasibility Market Fit Technical Complexity
3
Go-to-Market Strategy
Complete commercial plan including use cases, buyer personas, sales enablement materials, and marketing content to equip the sales team.
Use Cases Competitors Target Personas
5
Executive Approval
Leadership Review & Alignment

The completed business case is reviewed by executive leadership for approval. The Chief Growth Officer evaluates market and revenue potential, while aligning with the Chief Technology Officer on technical feasibility, resource allocation, and platform architecture fit.

Approval AuthorityChief Growth Officer
Technical AlignmentChief Technology Officer
6
Outcome
Roadmap Prioritization & Backlog Placement

Approved modules are prioritized into the product roadmap and development backlog based on their weighted ROI score, strategic alignment, and resource availability. The module enters the build pipeline with a clear scope, timeline, and go-to-market plan ready for execution.
